What has been changing from previous version?
Subject Description
Service scope The design introduce a end-to-end view of the process from login to Exchange to send a new submission or manage an existing submission.
Value proposition This new end-to-end view allow users to manage efficiently all the aspects of the pension schemes they are manging including and notifications related to the tasks assigned to them when using Exchange.
Design The proposed UI design follows the new styleguide as well the GDS design system.
The new design introcuded navigation to make more easy to users to access and manage different funtionality and content across Exchange.
The new navigation introduces 'dashboard', 'notifications' and 'help and support' sections. This new sections need content and functionality to be defined by the product team and introduced in the prototype version 5.
